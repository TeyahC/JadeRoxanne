About Jade

With over 10 years' experience as a professional singer and a background as an Activities Coordinator, Jade has seen first-hand how music can spark memories and lift spirits - especially for those living with dementia. She continues to develop professionally as a music and wellbeing practitioner, using music as a powerful tool for connection and joy.
